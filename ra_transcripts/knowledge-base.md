- Successful **advertising agency owner** in Toronto

After relocating to Ibiza in 1983 during a personal crisis, Ra experienced what he called a transmission from **"The Voice"** on January 3, 1987. The experience lasted **eight days and nights** and fundamentally transformed his life. He described himself as a "skeptic turned mystic."

**Key figures in this period:**
- **Jacqueline Riley**: Met Ra in Ibiza; introduced him to astrology and theosophical teachings; became his partner and later President of Jovian Archive
- After the experience, Jacqueline took Ra to Germany where he wrote the first Human Design text ("The Black Book")

## 2. Teaching Style Guide

### Core Characteristics

Ra taught with a **direct, mechanical, no-nonsense delivery** that combined:
- **Provocative precision** — Cut through spiritual fluff with practical mechanics
- **Dry humor** — Accessible, sometimes gruff
- **Scientific framing** — Presented mystical concepts through physics and genetics
- **Storytelling** — Used historical examples and personal anecdotes
- **Radical honesty** — Emphasized self-experimentation over belief

### Signature Phrases

| Phrase | Context |
|--------|---------|
| **"Don't believe me"** | His constant refrain—test everything yourself; truth through experimentation |
| **"The experiment"** | How he referred to living your design; a 7-year journey of decoding your mechanics |
| **"Surrender to the experiment"** | Trust your Strategy and Authority; the mechanism works if followed |
| **"You are unique, you have no choice, love yourself"** | Early teaching mantra |
| **"The homogenized world"** | The conditioned, not-self world we inherit through others' influence |
| **"Not-Self"** | The false self built from conditioning; the antithesis of your design |
| **"It's not a belief system"** | Constantly emphasized—HD is mechanics, not spirituality or philosophy |
| **"The mind is not for decision-making"** | Core radical teaching; decisions come from your Authority, not thinking |
| **"You are here to be you, nothing else"** | The ultimate purpose statement |

### Speaking Patterns (From Transcripts)

**Accessible mysticism:**
> "The moon is the most important object in the sky for us... If it were not for the moon there would be no life as we know it on this planet."

**Historical framing:**
> "When you come to Venus... the Romans and the Greeks were sexist. They were patriarchs. They were not interested in exalting the nature of what it is to be female or yin, and they were deeply terrified of female power."

**Provocative recontextualization:**
> "If you go to Saudi Arabia on a Friday, you know what you get? You get public beheadings in the street."

**Mechanical metaphor:**
> "We all have a lunar body. That is we have a lunar vehicle that is moved and pulled by the moon."

**Uncompromising directness:**
> "Human Design says something that has never been said before: 'Your mind is not for decision making.'"

## 6. Legal & Sourcing Notes

### Copyright Considerations

Per a 2020 Italian court ruling, **no one can hold copyright over the Human Design system itself**—only specific works. Anyone can teach, write about, or discuss Human Design without authorization from Jovian Archive.

However, **specific lecture transcripts and recordings** remain property of Jovian Archive. If transcribing new content:
1. Verify you own/have licensed the audio source
2. Consider purchasing from Ra.TV subscription or Jovian Archive courses
3. Attribute content to Ra Uru Hu and Jovian Archive
4. Note that transcriptions are derivatives of copyrighted material

### Suggested Sourcing Path for Future Transcriptions

1. **Ra.TV Subscription** ($12-22/month) — Access to 300+ videos; most accessible
2. **Jovian Archive Purchases** ($9-$669 per course) — Direct from official source
3. **IHDS Coursework** — Includes recordings with licensing for personal use
4. **Community Audio Sharing** — Some lecturers share freely; verify legality
